The Challenge

Breaking Down Barriers

Understanding the critical communication challenges faced by the deaf community in Bahrain and the Middle East region

Communication Challenges

Over 5,000 deaf individuals in Bahrain face daily difficulties communicating, which affects social interaction, access to services, and everyday activities.

Education Gaps

Limited learning support and a lack of sign language interpreters make it harder for deaf students to succeed in school and build skills.

Employment Challenges

Communication barriers in the workplace reduce job opportunities and lead to higher unemployment among the deaf community.

Solution

The AXIS Solution

A simple, fast, and private way to enable instant communication between deaf and hearing individuals

Simple. Fast. Accessible.

AXIS is an AI-powered smart glove that translates Arabic Sign Language into spoken output in real time, allowing deaf and hearing users to communicate instantly without barriers.

By combining lightweight sensors with efficient AI processing, AXIS delivers communication that is significantly faster and more affordable than existing solutions — without relying on cloud-based speech synthesis or external data processing.

  • Real-time Arabic Sign Language recognition
  • Instant communication with no noticeable delay
  • 87% cheaper than existing market alternatives
  • Up to 300× faster response time compared to traditional systems
  • 100% private — no user data stored or shared
  • Lightweight, comfortable design for everyday use
  • Works offline with preconfigured audio responses

Technology

AI-Driven Intelligence

Efficient AI models designed for fast, reliable, real-time sign language interpretation

Real-Time Gesture Intelligence

AXIS uses a custom-built AI model designed specifically to process sensor data from sign language gestures in real time. The system focuses on speed, accuracy, and reliability while running on lightweight hardware.

By applying optimized machine-learning techniques and model quantization, AXIS delivers instant responses with minimal processing overhead, making it both fast and energy-efficient without compromising user privacy.

  • Optimized machine-learning model for real-time sensor data processing
  • Random Forest–based classification for reliable gesture recognition
  • Quantized models for faster performance on low-power devices
  • Edge-based processing with no cloud dependency
  • Consistent accuracy with ultra-low response time
